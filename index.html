Chill Sunday. Kings Park.

Kings Park & Botanic Garden

Day 03 / 10

Mon · 22 Jun

Rottnest. Bikes and quokkas.

Quokka, Rottnest Island

Train to Freo, ferry to the island, bike the loop. Car-free island, ~22km loop, mostly flat. Quokkas all over the settlement.

  • 07:30 Train Perth → Fremantle (30 min)
  • 08:30 Ferry from B Shed (25 min)
  • 09:15 Bike rental at Pedal & Flipper
  • 10:00 Loop: Pinky Beach, Bathurst Lighthouse, The Basin
  • 12:30 Lunch at Hotel Rottnest
  • 16:30 Ferry back, train to Perth
bookRottnest Express costferry + bike around A$110 pp
No selfies holding quokkas. Squat low, let them come to you.

Day 04 / 10

Tue · 23 Jun

Yanchep National Park.

Koalas at Yanchep NP

Train north on the new Yanchep Line, ~50 minutes, A$5. Koala boardwalk, Crystal Cave guided tour, kangaroos round Loch McNess.

  • 09:30 Yanchep Line, Perth Underground to Yanchep
  • 10:30 Bus 491 or short Uber to the NP entrance
  • 11:00 Koala Boardwalk + Loch McNess walk loop
  • 13:00 Crystal Cave guided tour (book ahead)
  • 14:30 Lunch at Chocolate Drops Cafe on site
  • 17:00 Back to Perth, dinner Long Chim (Thai)
costTrain A$5, cave A$25 stayPerth CBD
Cave tours run 11:00, 12:00, 13:30, 14:30. Book the day before.

Day 05 / 10

Wed · 24 Jun

Coach south to Margaret River.

Margaret River vineyards in winter

Pack a weekender, leave the big bags at the Perth hotel. Catch the Transwa SW1 coach. Arrive in time for an afternoon stroll and a fireplace cellar door.

  • 08:30 Check out, walk to Esplanade Busport
  • 09:30 Transwa SW1 coach, Perth → Margaret River
  • 14:30 Arrive MR, check into hotel
  • 15:30 Margaret River Chocolate Co., free tastings
  • 17:00 Fireplace cellar door, Vasse Felix lounge
  • 19:30 Dinner at Morries
coach~5 h · around A$70 pp stayMargaret River town (2 nights)
Book SW1 ahead on transwa.wa.gov.au. Be at the busport 15 min early.

Day 06 / 10

Thu · 25 Jun

Whales, truffles, the works.

Humpbacks & southern rights, Flinders Bay

The one full MR day. June is peak for whales AND start of truffle season. Hotel pickup both halves. Pub dinner to recover.

  • 08:30 Shuttle to Augusta (~40 min south)
  • 10:30 Whale watching, Naturaliste Charters (2½ h)
  • 13:30 Light lunch, back to MR area
  • 15:00 Truffle hunt with dogs + tastings, Forage Safaris
  • 19:00 Dinner at Settler’s Tavern (pub, fire going)
bookNaturaliste + Truffle Farm cost~A$95 whales · ~A$95 truffle
Pack rain shell. Boat is wet either way. Bring seasickness tablets, take 30 min before.

  • 15:00 Collect big bags, train Perth → Fremantle (30 min)

stayFremantle (3 nights) transitcoach + train, A$5 train

June weather

  • Winter. Perth 11 to 18°C. MR 13 to 17°C.
  • Wet down south. 15 to 18 rainy days in MR.
  • Sea is around 17°C. Look, don’t swim.
  • Sunrise ~07:15, sunset ~17:25.

Pack list

  • Proper rain shell, not a windbreaker.
  • Warm fleece + thermal layer for boat days.
  • Walking shoes (Rottnest, Yanchep).
  • Weekender bag for MR (big bags stay in Perth).
  • AU 3-pin adapter (Type I).

Book before you leave

  • Hotels: Perth (4 nights), MR town (2), Freo (3).
  • Transwa SW1 coach, both ways.
  • Rottnest Express ferry + bike, day 3.
  • Crystal Cave Yanchep, day 4.
  • Whale watching + truffle hunt, day 6.
  • Long Chim, Wildflower (2-3 wk lead).

SIM & pay

  • Tap to pay everywhere. No cash needed.
  • Optus 28-day Travel SIM at the airport.
  • Get a SmartRider card on day 1 (PTA), tap on/off for trains.
  • Download Google Maps offline before flying.

Numbers to keep

  • Emergency, 000.
  • Non-emergency police, 131 444.
  • Transwa coach, 1300 662 205.

Two hotel moves.

No car, no chaos. Only twice on the whole trip do the big bags actually go anywhere.

  1. Sat 20 Land Perth. Airport train to Perth Station. Check in Perth hotel. Big bags arrive.
  2. Wed 24 Walk to Esplanade Busport with a weekender only. SW1 coach to MR. Big bags stay at Perth hotel.
  3. Fri 26 SW1 coach back. Collect big bags at Perth hotel. Train to Fremantle, check in. Big bags second move.
  4. Mon 29 Uber Freo to airport. Done.

Day trips from base: Rottnest via Freo (day 3), Yanchep NP via train (day 4), Cottesloe + S Freo (day 9). All return same day, no luggage involved.
